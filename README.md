# IkerCraft: Aeronautics

A Minecraft modpack focused on flight, exploration, and engineering. Take to the skies and build your way to the clouds!

---

## About

**IkerCraft: Aeronautics** is a modpack centered around aviation, airships, and aerial exploration. Whether you want to soar through the skies in a custom aircraft, build floating bases, or explore the world from above, this pack has everything you need to get off the ground.

---

## Features

- **Flight & Aviation** - Craft and pilot planes, airships, and other flying machines
- **Aerial Exploration** - Discover new biomes and structures from the sky
- **Engineering & Building** - Design complex flying contraptions and sky bases
- **Performance Optimized** - Smooth gameplay even at high altitudes

---

## Installation

1. Download and install [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/) or [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/)
2. Search for **IkerCraft: Aeronautics** or import the modpack file manually
3. Launch the modpack and enjoy!

> **Recommended RAM:** 4-6 GB
